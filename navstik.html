Design Internship at NavStik Autonomous systems, Pune





About my work

This was my first design internship after my graduation in engineering. I was very new to the field and was getting my hands dirty with every kind of design project. I worked on varied design projects like product design of drones and MAVs to test NavStik autopilot hardware, working on a wiki website to make using NavStik hardware easy, making rigs to simulate in-flight conditions of an MAV and various graphic design projects during my internship.

I had immense fun working on all the projects at NavStik. I was new the domain and was learning every single day!
